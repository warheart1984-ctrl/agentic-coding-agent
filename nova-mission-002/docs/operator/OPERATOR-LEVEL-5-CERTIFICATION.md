A Level 5 Constitutional Runtime Designer must demonstrate mastery in:

- Designing PIT-band evolution rules
- Engineering new constitutional objects
- Designing lawful action predicates for new kernels
- Designing continuity proofs and lineage systems
- Designing multi-agent constitutional coherence protocols
- Designing constitutional upgrade paths that preserve continuity
- Designing distributed constitutional consensus protocols
- Designing constitutional panic-handling and recovery systems

Level 5 operators are constitutional kernel designers.
