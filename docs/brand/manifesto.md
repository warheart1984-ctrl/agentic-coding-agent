# Nova Brand Manifesto

Code should obey law.  
Autonomy should answer to sovereignty.  
Intelligence should be governed, not unleashed.

Nova exists to make that real.

We believe that every autonomous action must be:

- Grounded in evidence
- Bound by a constitution
- Reversible at any moment
- Auditable by design
- Approved by the human in charge

Nova is not just a tool.  
It is a covenant between engineers and their systems.

We stand for:

- **Governance** over chaos
- **Structure** over improvisation
- **Provenance** over mystery
- **Sovereignty** over automation

Nova is where code becomes a citizen of a governed world.  
Where engineering is not just powerful—  
but accountable.
