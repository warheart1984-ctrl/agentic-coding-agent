# Full Cinematic Animation Storyboard — "NOVA: THE GOVERNED ENGINE"

---

## Scene 1 — The Void

> Black screen.
> A faint hum.
> A single point of blue light flickers.

**Narrator (calm, resonant):**  
In the silence before creation… governance awakens.

The point expands into a crystalline core.

---

## Scene 2 — The Constitutional Core

> The core unfolds like a celestial machine.
> Six luminous layers rise around it.

- **Gold slab:** Constitutional Layer
- **Blue slab:** Cognitive Layer
- **Cyan slab:** Code Intelligence Layer
- **White slab:** Runtime Layer
- **Violet slab:** Integration Layer
- **Black slab:** Interface Layer

Each layer rotates, locking into place.

**Narrator:**  
Where autonomy meets sovereignty… Nova is born.

---

## Scene 3 — The Planner

> A holographic architect stands before the core.
> Pages of the Plan Contract assemble in midair.

**Narrator:**  
The Planner writes the law.

---

## Scene 4 — The Executor

> Mechanical arms of light apply patches to glowing code.
> Diffs ripple like neon waves.

**Narrator:**  
The Executor enforces it.

---

## Scene 5 — The Debugger

> A storm of red error glyphs.
> The Debugger descends like a judge, issuing corrective beams.

**Narrator:**  
The Debugger interprets failure.

---

## Scene 6 — The Refiner

> A human silhouette steps forward.
> The system bows.
> A patch hovers, awaiting approval.

**Narrator:**  
And the User… rules above all.

---

## Scene 7 — The Git Fractal

> Branches explode outward like fractal geometry.
> A PR portal opens, shimmering.

---

## Scene 8 — The Crest

> The Nova Crest ignites in gold and blue.

**Narrator:**  
This is Nova.  
Governance. Evidence. Sovereignty.

> Fade out.
