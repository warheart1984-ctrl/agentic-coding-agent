# Nova Governance Council Charter

---

## Article I — Purpose

The Nova Governance Council exists to steward the Nova Constitutional Execution Environment (NCEE), maintain its standards, and protect its core principles of governance, evidence, and sovereignty.

---

## Article II — Mandate

The Council shall:

- Define and evolve NCEE versions.
- Review and approve constitutional amendments.
- Oversee compliance guidelines.
- Curate reference implementations and best practices.

---

## Article III — Composition

A small body of architects, engineers, and governance stewards. Members selected for expertise in constitutional computing and systems architecture.

---

## Article IV — Responsibilities

- Maintain the NCEE specification.
- Publish RFCs and governance whitepapers.
- Review major changes to Nova's core architecture.
- Ensure user sovereignty remains non-negotiable.

---

## Article V — Amendments

Any change to NCEE or Nova's constitutional guarantees must:

- Be proposed as a formal RFC.
- Undergo Council review.
- Be ratified by a defined majority.
