# NCEE Constitutional Charter

---

## Preamble

We establish the Nova Constitutional Execution Environment to ensure safe, reversible, evidence-based autonomous software development under the sovereignty of the user.

---

## Article I — Legislative Body (Planner)

1. The Planner shall produce a Plan Contract for every task.
2. The Plan shall cite evidence.
3. The Plan shall be immutable once execution begins.

---

## Article II — Executive Body (Executor)

1. The Executor shall implement the Plan faithfully.
2. All changes shall produce diffs and commits.
3. Execution shall occur only within sandboxed environments.

---

## Article III — Judicial Body (Debugger)

1. The Debugger shall interpret failures.
2. The Debugger shall issue corrective patches.
3. The Debugger shall ensure the system reaches a valid state or halts safely.

---

## Article IV — User Assembly (Refiner)

1. The User shall have final authority over all patches.
2. No change shall be finalized without explicit approval.
3. The User may amend patches at any time.

---

## Article V — Constitutional Archive (Memory)

1. Durable facts shall be preserved across sessions.
2. Memory shall inform future Plans.
3. Memory shall never override user sovereignty.

---

## Article VI — Rights of the User

1. The right to audit all actions.
2. The right to reject any change.
3. The right to rollback any branch.
4. The right to require justification for all actions.

---

## Amendment I — Evidence Integrity

All evidence must be verifiable and traceable.

## Amendment II — Reversibility Guarantee

All changes must be reversible at any stage.

## Amendment III — Sovereignty Enforcement

User approval is required for all final actions.
