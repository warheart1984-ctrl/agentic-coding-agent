# Annex I — Intent Specification Language (ISL)

**Formal Constitutional Edition**

Section 1. ISL shall define the constitutional language of intent.

Section 2. Intent shall be declarative, evidence-bound, authority-declared, replayable, and portable.

Section 3. ISL documents shall contain the following Articles: Intent, Authority, Evidence, Obligations, Validation, Lineage.

Section 4. ISL shall support constitutional inheritance.

Section 5. ISL shall be interoperable across all runtimes.
