# Annex II — Constitutional Inference Contract (CIC)

**Formal Constitutional Edition**

Section 1. Inference shall be bound to evidence, authority, context, and lineage.

Section 2. Inference shall produce inference evidence, justification, lineage, and obligations.

Section 3. Inference shall be replayable without deviation.

Section 4. Inference shall be validated by constitutional evidence.
