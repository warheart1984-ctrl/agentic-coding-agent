# Annex VI — Arena Certification Protocol (ACP)

**Formal Constitutional Edition**

Section 1. Arenas shall be certified by constitutional evidence.

Section 2. Certification shall require structural, authority, validation, and lineage evidence.

Section 3. Certification shall be replayable.

Section 4. Certified arenas shall uphold constitutional invariants.
