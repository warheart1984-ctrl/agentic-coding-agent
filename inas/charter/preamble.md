# Preamble

We establish the Implementation-Neutral Assurance Specification to define the constitutional substrate upon which governed intelligence shall operate.

We affirm that:

- **Evidence** is the foundation of authority
- **Assurance** is the measure of fidelity
- **Conformance** is the condition of legitimacy
- **Interoperability** is the guarantee of federation
- **Lineage** is the preservation of truth

We declare that this specification shall remain **neutral, sovereign, and binding** across all runtimes, vendors, architectures, and worlds.

No runtime may override, reinterpret, or redefine these rules.
