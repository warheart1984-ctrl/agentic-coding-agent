# Mythar Constitutional Ratification v0.3

This amendment resolves three v0.2 candidate lexemes without changing the frozen v0.2 contract.

| Lexeme | Ratified category | Constitutional role | Evidence used |
|---|---|---|---|
| `ema` | Root | beautiful existence; target of `ra-` in `raema` | Semantic Engine example: `ra + ema` → amplified beauty/existence. |
| `fak` | Particle | craft/making frame; stackable but non-governing | Grammar example `ja fak la ma` labels `fak` as CRAFT. |
| `tila` | Meta-Operator | postfix purity/refinement transformation | Modifier example `maia tila` is glossed “the pure spring goddess.” |

`raema` and `maia` are canonical v0.3 composites. `tila` must immediately follow the expression it transforms.
